What's this place?

Welcome to snail.rocks, the place where I dump my shaders and tools.

I really enjoy making shaders! People ask for them to put on worlds, avatars or just to see how they work. I wanted to get a single place to put things. This is a bit less structured than I would like, but it exists at all which is better than nothing!

Help out by sharing pictures or fixes to these! Contact information is in the menu at the top!
